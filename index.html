About Me

Hi, I’m Aryan Singh — a full-stack dreamer, code-crafter, and digital problem solver based in Kolkata. With strong command over PHP, Laravel, WordPress, and MySQL, I build websites that are not only visually engaging but also optimized for real-world performance.

My journey into web development wasn’t linear — and I’m proud of that. I began in business development at NoBroker Technologies, where I honed my understanding of user needs and client expectations. That role sparked my deeper interest in tech, which led me to pivot into web development full-time.

From crafting lead-generating WordPress pages to building dynamic Laravel applications, I’ve worn many hats — developer, designer, problem solver, and team collaborator. At Vercis Infotech, I built custom WordPress features and REST API integrations. Later, at IconicPages, I focused on backend development, database design, and clean UI with Blade templating.

I hold a B.Tech in Computer Science (CGPA: 8.97) and a Diploma in Computer Science (79%). Currently, I’m pursuing my M.Tech at G H Raisoni College of Engineering & Management, Pune.

Beyond coding, I’m proud to serve as a member of Lions Club International, combining tech with purpose. I believe the best solutions are crafted at the intersection of creativity, logic, and empathy — and that’s exactly where I like to work.
